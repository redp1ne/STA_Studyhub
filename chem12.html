Chemistry 12

Provincial-exam style preparation organized by unit, difficulty, and topic.

Unit Overview

  • Unit 1 – Reaction Kinetics
    Rates, collision theory, rate laws.
  • Unit 2 – Chemical Equilibrium
    Le Châtelier, Kc, ICE tables.
  • Unit 3 – Solubility Equilibrium
    Ksp, precipitation, complex ions.
  • Unit 4 – Acids & Bases
    pH, Ka/Kb, buffers, titrations.

Study Strategy (Mentor Tips)

These suggestions are based on students who scored 95%+ in Chemistry 12.

  1. Start with unit summaries, then move to targeted Provincial questions.
  2. Re-do questions you got wrong and write out why the right answer is correct.
  3. Practice mixed sets near test day to simulate exam conditions.
