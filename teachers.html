Information for Teachers

A simple, optional way to share lesson recordings, extra notes, and clarification materials.

How Teacher Accounts Work

  • Accounts are created and verified by the site administrator.
  • Teachers can upload links to recorded lessons (e.g., Teams, YouTube unlisted, Loom).
  • Teachers can attach PDFs of extra notes, review packages, or answer keys they choose to share.
  • All content follows school academic integrity and copyright expectations.

Example Uses

  • Posting a “Unit 3 Review” video for students who missed class.
  • Sharing a simplified summary sheet for complex topics.
  • Clarifying common mistakes seen on quizzes or tests.

Planned Features

Upload Portal

Future feature: secure login-based upload portal, where teachers can attach files or links. For now, links to resources can be added manually by the student admin.

Per-Course Teacher Sections

Each course page (e.g., Chemistry 12, Pre-Calculus 12) can include a “From the Teacher” section with official guidance.
