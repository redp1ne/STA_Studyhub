Pre-Calculus 12

Functions, graphs, algebra, and exam-style practice organized by unit.

Unit Overview

  • Unit 1 – Polynomial Functions
    End behaviour, graphing, inequalities, and factoring review.
  • Unit 2 – Rational Functions
    Asymptotes, restrictions, and graph analysis.
  • Unit 3 – Exponential & Logarithmic Functions
    Growth/decay, laws of logs, solving equations.
  • Unit 4 – Trigonometry
    Trig equations, identities, graphs, and applications.
  • Unit 5 – Combinatorics & Binomial Theorem
    Permutations, combinations, Pascal’s triangle, binomial expansion.

Study Strategy (Mentor Tips)

From students who scored 95%+ in Pre-Calc 12.

  1. Summarize each unit on one page: key formulas, graphs, and “typical question types”.
  2. Do mixed review sets instead of only section-by-section questions near test time.
  3. When you get a question wrong, write down what kind of mistake it was (algebra, concept, graph reading, etc.).
