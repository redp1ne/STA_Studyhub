About STA Study Hub

A student-led project to make test preparation more organized, fair, and accessible.

Purpose

STA Study Hub was created to bring together high-quality notes, mentor tips, and exam-style practice in one place. Instead of every student searching for their own resources, we centralize and organize the best materials available.

Content Standards

  • Resources are contributed by students who have demonstrated strong performance (95%+ where possible).
  • All student submissions are reviewed by an administrator before publishing.
  • No copyrighted test materials (e.g., current teacher-made tests) are uploaded.
  • Resources are meant to support learning, not to replace paying attention in class.

Future Development

Over time, the goal is to expand the number of supported courses, integrate secure teacher and student logins, and build a fully interactive upload and moderation system.
