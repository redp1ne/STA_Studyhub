Physics 12

Advanced forces, energy, momentum, and electricity preparation for tests and final exams.

Unit Overview

  • Unit 1 – Vectors & 2D Kinematics
    Vector components, relative motion, and projectiles.
  • Unit 2 – Dynamics in 2D
    Inclined planes, circular motion, multi-force systems.
  • Unit 3 – Work, Energy & Power
    Advanced conservation problems and non-conservative forces.
  • Unit 4 – Momentum & Collisions
    2D momentum, explosions, and impulse.
  • Unit 5 – Electricity
    Electric fields, potential, circuits (series/parallel), and Ohm’s law.

Study Strategy (Mentor Tips)

From students who took Physics 12 and scored 90–95%+.

  1. Start every problem by listing knowns/unknowns and drawing a clear diagram.
  2. Practice a mix of conceptual questions and heavy-calculation questions.
  3. Time yourself on small sets of 3–5 problems to simulate test pressure.
