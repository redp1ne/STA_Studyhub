Physics 11

Introductory kinematics, dynamics, energy, and waves with example-based practice.

Unit Overview

  • Unit 1 – Kinematics
    Position-time and velocity-time graphs, constant acceleration, motion in one dimension.
  • Unit 2 – Dynamics
    Forces, free-body diagrams, Newton’s laws, friction.
  • Unit 3 – Energy & Work
    Kinetic and potential energy, work, power, conservation of energy.
  • Unit 4 – Momentum
    Impulse, conservation of momentum, collisions.
  • Unit 5 – Waves & Optics (if covered)
    Wave properties, reflection, refraction, lenses.

Study Strategy (Mentor Tips)

How past high-scoring students approached Physics 11.

  1. Draw diagrams for almost every problem, even simple ones.
  2. Write formulas with units and check if your final answer’s units make sense.
  3. Redo quiz/test questions you missed and write a one-sentence explanation of the concept behind each.
